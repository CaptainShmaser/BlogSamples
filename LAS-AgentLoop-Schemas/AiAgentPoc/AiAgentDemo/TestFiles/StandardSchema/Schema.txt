PaymentID (String): Unique identifier for the payment.
LoanID (String): Reference to the loan for which the payment was made.
PaymentDate (Date): Date of the payment (format: YYYY-MM-DD).
PaymentAmount (Decimal): Amount paid.
PaymentMethod (String): Method of payment (e.g., Credit Card, Bank Transfer, Cash).
LoanBalance (Decimal): Remaining balance on the loan after the payment.
